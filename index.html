Yuqi Tan

I'm a PhD Candidate in Biochemistry, Cellular and Molecular Biology working in Cahan Lab. I have developed SingleCellNet , a computational tool to classify single cell RNA seq data across platform and across species.

Currently, I am exploring mouse gastrulation, assessing cell fate engineering protocols & investiagting osteosarcoma heterogeneity at a single cell resolution.

I will be presenting singleCellNet at ISSCR 2019 consession IVE (14:24 - 14:35pm, Jun29, Room 408B).
